## Key Insights

### Billa Legume Strategy
1. **Canned legumes are the protein king** - BILLA and clever store brands offer 28-60 Kč/kg when on sale
2. **"Sleva" is your friend** - Sale items often lack direct price but show excellent unit pricing
3. **Dried vs Canned**:
   - Canned: 29-97 Kč/kg, ready-to-eat, 2-3yr shelf life
   - Dried: 60-140 Kč/kg, requires cooking, indefinite shelf life
   - Break-even: ~2kg/month consumption favors dried
4. **Premium brands cost 2-3x** - Bonduelle, Giana, Heinz charge 2-7x for similar products
5. **Package size matters** - Larger cans (540g, 855g) better unit price than small (240g)

## Storage & Preparation
- **Canned legumes**: Ready to eat, 2-3 year shelf life, rinse before use to reduce sodium
- **Dried legumes**: Require soaking (8-12h) + cooking (30-90 min), indefinite shelf life when dry and cool
- **Economic break-even**: Dried legumes worth it for regular consumption (>2kg/month)
- **Convenience factor**: Canned worth premium for time-poor households despite 2x cost
